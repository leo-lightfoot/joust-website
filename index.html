ABOUT JIIT


Jaypee Institute of Information Technology is an institute of higher education deemed to be university in Sector 62, Noida. It has 7 academic departments and it offers programs in technical education at undergraduate, postgraduate and doctorate level.

The college is known for its unmatched academic curricula and a plethora of extracurricular activities which provide all round holistic development of each and every student. With several hubs managed by the students themselves which focus on nurturing various skills and talents along with inculcating knowledge in fields as diverse as dance, music, theatre, literature, robotics, game development, multimedia, coding, fitness and the arts, JIIT has a lot to offer. There are also several student run Non Governmental Organisations in this college that have pledged their contribution to environmental and social causes.

The college campus boasts of world class infrastructure, high tech laboratories and equipment, a modern Learning Resource Centre, and other amenities. The campus has a vibrant ambience with ample opportunities for students and mentors to grow and thrive alike.

ABOUT THE JOUST


The Joust is the Literary Festival of Jaypee Institute of Information Technology, Sector 62, Noida organised by Parola-The Literary Hub. Since its inception in 2015, it has earned a name for itself in the Literary Circuit of Delhi NCR. Referring to the medieval sport of lancing, the word 'Joust' signifies the verbal battle that takes place in the various events organised during the Literary Festival.

A one-of-a-kind platform for each one of us to discover our potential and showcase our opinions by indulging in activities such as debate, poetry, creative writing as well as MUNs. Stimulating the young minds to think beyond the norm, The Joust is a platform for students to connect with issues that concern the real world and affect the society they're a part of. It helps them find their potential and skills on how to go about discussing these issues, writing about them and coming up with feasible solutions to these real world problems

Now in it's 4th edition, The Joust is sure to surpass its past editions and leave its mark as one of the best literary extravaganza held so far.

LEGACY OF JOUST

The first edition of Joust was organised in 2015, with the aim to create a new platform for debaters, poets, writers and photographers. The Joust was meant to rekindle the spirit of Jaypee Youth Parliament, and expand its horizons to include other events apart from committee negotiations. The Joust 2016 went a step further, comprising of five major events. The Literary Festival reached its peak in 2017 in its third edition, with 7 events, 300 participants and a stellar Executive Board. We aim to surpass all the previous editions with The Joust 2018.

QUALITY EVENTS

The Joust 2018 comprises of 7 highly engaging events. It will have 4 unique committees, a multi-format Hindi debate, a slam poetry competition, a creative fiction competition and a press event. All the events will be presided over by highly experienced Executive Board members and judges. With exciting prizes for the winners, the Joust has a motto of quality over quantity. This edition is going to set a benchmark of quality debate and discussion with a huge number of debaters, MUNers, poets, writers and photographers from across the country participating.

HOSPITALITY

The Joust has an extremely dedicated and hard working organising committee committed to make the Literary Festival an unparalleld experience for its participants. With over 40 members distributed across 6 major departments, the team has pledged to fully dedicate its time to make this event bigger than ever before, in terms of participation, quality, judges as well as an overall experience. The team has been highly regarded in the past for being helpful and hospitable to the delegates and the team hopes to continue that trend this year as well.
